Privacy Policy

Session Draft does not collect, transmit, or store user data on external servers.

All session reflections and related content are stored locally on your device.

Session Draft does not have access to any content entered into the app.

The app is designed to support general session reflection and workflow preparation and is not intended to replace official clinical documentation systems or electronic health records.

When exporting or sharing content, users are responsible for ensuring compliance with their applicable privacy and data handling obligations.

No analytics, tracking, or third-party data collection services are used.
